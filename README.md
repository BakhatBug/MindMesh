**Connecting Minds, Creating Knowledge**

MindMesh is a dedicated peer-to-peer knowledge exchange platform designed specifically for the academic community. Unlike general social media, MindMesh provides a structured, AI-moderated environment where students, teachers, and researchers can collaborate without the noise of irrelevant content.

---

## ‚ú® Key Features
* **Verified Academic Space:** Exclusively for university/college members.
* **AI-Powered Moderation:** Uses LLMs (Gemini/Mistral) to automatically flag non-academic or offensive content.
* **Reputation System:** Users earn "Contribution Scores" based on the quality of their discussions and endorsements.
* **Threaded Discussions:** Organized by sub-domains/fields to keep knowledge structured.
* **Direct Messaging:** Secure peer-to-peer communication for deeper collaboration.
